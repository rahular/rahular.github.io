Rahul Aralikatte

I am a Ph.D student in the CoAStaL NLP group at the University of Copenhagen working with Prof. Anders Søgaard. My area of research is Natural Language Understanding. Recently I have been working on improving coreference and SRL models jointly, generating faithful summaries, and efficient transfer in low-resource settings. I am funded by a Google Focused Research Award.
